## how to get url link for USSD code provider 

### 1. [login into your Account](https://shortcodeafrica.com/get-started)


### 2. Click on Get Started…
![Step 2 screenshot](/assets/images/url/url.png)


### 3. Click on My Apps
![Step 3 screenshot](/assets/images/url/url1.png)


### 4. Selecte from list of App	…
![Step 4 screenshot](/assets/images/url/url2.png)


### 5. Click on the blue icon App Settings
![Step 5 screenshot](/assets/images/url/url3.png)


### 6. select your prefer Application Url ( Test or Production )
![Step 6 screenshot](/assets/images/url/url4.png)

![Step 7 screenshot](/assets/images/url/url5.png)
